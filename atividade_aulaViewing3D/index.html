Camera Controls

  • Move (Translation):
    • W: Move Forward
    • S: Move Backward
    • A: Strafe Left
    • D: Strafe Right
    • Q: Move Up
    • E: Move Down
  • Rotate (World Rotation):
    • ← (Left Arrow): Rotate Left (Y-Axis)
    • → (Right Arrow): Rotate Right (Y-Axis)
